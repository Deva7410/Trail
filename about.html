About CET

CET is housed in a separate massive three storeyed building (Vishweshraiya Bhawan) in the University Campus itself. It has state of the art laboratories of Computer, Electronics, Mechanical Engineering, Engineering Drawing, Physics, Chemistry, Electrical Machine lab etc.

A Separate workshop of the college has been constructed in the campus, which is fully equipped with the machines, other infrastructure & generator. Learned and experienced faculties have been appointed in all the branches. Sufficient number of supporting staff has also been appointed for smooth functioning of the college. Facilities of latest teaching aid and technology are made available. Moreover CET has digital campus with internet & wi-fi facilities and has a rich library.

Admission Procedure and ELEGIBILITY

1. B.Tech:(Through JCECE of JCECE Board)- First Semester/First Year

Admission into First Semester/First Year of B.Tech. course through Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination (JCECE) of Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) of Jharkhand Govt.

2. D to D ( Through D to DECE of JCECE Board) - Third Semester/Second Year

Admission into 3rd Semester/2nd Year (Lateral Entry) of B.Tech. Courses through Diploma to Degree Entrance Competitive Examination (DtoDECE) of Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) of Jharkhand Govt.
