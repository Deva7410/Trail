The Department offers instructions at undergraduate level with the aim of providing a sound background in the areas of Mechanical Engineering.

B.Tech Programmes [duration : 4 years/8 semester]

Bachelor of Technology [B.Tech.] is a four year undergraduate program. The Department of Mechanical Engineering at CET Hazaribagh is renowned for imparting state of the art undergraduate education. We attract the brightest students from the state and country who score very high ranks in the entrance exam for any academic institution..

Every year about 60 students are admitted for programme. The department offers B. Tech, with a wide choice of specializations, electives areas.
